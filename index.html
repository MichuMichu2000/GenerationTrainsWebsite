Experience the best simulation indie game about trains!

Generation Trains (formerly Neotrax) is a new simulation game/experience(in Early Access) in Roblox made by Chojrak Developement. Here, you can control a train and ride on a very large map. Explore the city, find a desert and relax when riding in a forest. Here are some attractions waiting for you:

  • More than 15 trains
  • Crossings, signals and rail junctions sounds
  • Possibility of reverse driving
  • Intuitive GUI to help you control your train
  • Stunning graphics and relaxing soundtrack
  • Camera settings
  • Train configuration
  • Feelings like in Rails Unlimited (the creator is helping us)
  • And more interesting details that we didn't wrote because of small space to write or are coming soon...

Remember playing with toy trains in childhood? Or want to know how it feels to drive a train? Then you don't have to wait anymore!

The game is still in Early Access, so you need to pay one-time fee in case to play(25 Robux for 20.01.2023).
